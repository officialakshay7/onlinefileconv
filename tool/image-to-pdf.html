Converting Images to PDF on Different Devices

How to Convert Image to PDF on iPhone

For iPhone users wondering how to convert image to PDF on iPhone, follow these steps:

  • Open the Photos app and select your image
  • Tap the Share icon and choose "Print"
  • Pinch outward on the preview to open the PDF version
  • Tap the Share icon again to save or send your PDF

How to Convert Image to PDF on Android

Android users can convert image to PDF using these steps:

  • Open the Files app and navigate to your image
  • Long-press on the image file
  • Select "Convert to PDF" from the menu
  • Save the new PDF file to your device
