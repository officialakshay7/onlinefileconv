Step by Step Guide to Delete Pages from a PDF

Everything you need to know about removing pages from PDF documents online

What is a PDF Page Remover?

A PDF page remover is a tool that lets you permanently delete one or more pages from a PDF document without needing Adobe Acrobat or any other paid software. Our free online delete PDF page tool processes everything in your browser — your file never leaves your device.

Why Delete Pages from a PDF?

There are many common reasons to remove pages from a PDF:

  • Remove Blank Pages: Scanner software often adds extra blank pages — delete them in seconds
  • Extract Relevant Content: Keep only the pages you need before sharing a document
  • Reduce File Size: Removing unnecessary pages makes the PDF smaller and easier to share
  • Clean Up Drafts: Remove draft pages, cover sheets, or placeholder pages before sending
  • Privacy: Remove pages that contain sensitive information before distributing a document
Pro Tip: Use the range selector to quickly select multiple pages at once. For example, type 1,3,5-8 to select pages 1, 3, and pages 5 through 8 in one step — much faster than clicking each thumbnail individually.

How to Delete Pages from a PDF Online?

Follow these simple steps to delete pages from your PDF using our free tool:

  • Upload your PDF using "Choose File" or drag and drop
  • All pages are rendered as thumbnails — click any page to mark it for deletion (highlighted in red)
  • Use the range selector to select multiple pages at once (e.g. 2-5, 8)
  • Use Select All or Deselect All for bulk actions
  • Click Delete Selected Pages to remove the chosen pages
  • Your updated PDF downloads automatically

Selecting Pages to Delete

Click to Select Individual Pages

Click any page thumbnail to toggle its selection on or off. Selected pages are highlighted with a red border and a trash icon overlay so you can clearly see which pages are marked for deletion before committing.

Using the Range Selector

The range selector lets you type a list of pages or ranges — for example 1, 3, 5-8, 12 — and apply that selection instantly. This is ideal for PDFs with many pages where clicking each one individually would take too long.

Select All / Deselect All

Use the Select All button to mark every page for deletion, or Deselect All to clear your selection and start again. Note: at least one page must remain in the PDF — the tool will warn you if you try to delete all pages.

Can I delete multiple pages from a PDF at once?

Yes! Click multiple page thumbnails to select them, or use the range input (e.g. 1, 3, 5-8) to select several pages at once. All selected pages are removed in a single step when you click "Delete Selected Pages".

You must keep at least one page in the PDF. If you select all pages for deletion, the tool will display a warning and prevent the download, as an empty PDF is not a valid document.
