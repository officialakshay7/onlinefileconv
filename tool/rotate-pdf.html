Step by Step Guide to Rotate PDF Pages

Everything you need to know about rotating pages in your PDF documents

What is a PDF Page Rotator?

A PDF page rotator is a tool that lets you change the orientation of pages in your PDF document - permanently. Whether a scanned document came out sideways, or you need to flip specific pages for better readability, our tool lets you rotate PDF pages online without any software installation.

Why Rotate PDF Pages?

There are several common reasons to rotate a PDF:

  • Scanned Documents: Scanned pages often come out rotated incorrectly - fix them in seconds
  • Mixed Orientation: Some PDFs have a mix of portrait and landscape pages that need aligning
  • Sharing & Printing: Ensure your PDF looks correct before sending it to clients or printing
  • Presentation: Rotate charts or wide tables from landscape back to portrait for cleaner documents
  • Mobile Scans: Photos or scans taken on a phone are often sideways or upside down
Pro Tip: Use the per-page rotation buttons (visible when you hover over a page thumbnail) to rotate individual pages without affecting the rest of the document - perfect for mixed-orientation PDFs.

How to Rotate PDF Pages Online?

Follow these simple steps to rotate PDF pages using our free tool:

  • Upload your PDF using "Choose File" or drag and drop
  • Preview all pages as thumbnails in the tool
  • Use Rotate All Pages to rotate every page at once, or click individual thumbnails to select specific pages
  • Click your desired rotation: 90° Clockwise, 180°, or 90° Counter-Clockwise
  • Click Apply & Download PDF to save your permanently rotated PDF

Rotating All Pages vs. Individual Pages

How to Rotate All Pages in a PDF

To rotate all pages in a PDF at once, use the "Rotate All Pages" buttons in the control panel. Choose 90° CW (clockwise), 180°, or 90° CCW (counter-clockwise). The rotation is shown live on the thumbnails before you download.

How to Rotate a Single PDF Page

To rotate a single page in a PDF, hover over any page thumbnail to reveal the rotation buttons, or click the thumbnail to select it and use the "Rotate Selected Pages" controls. Only the pages you select will be rotated in the output PDF.

Did You Know? Our rotate PDF free tool runs entirely in your browser using PDF.js and PDF-lib - your document is never uploaded to any server. Your files stay completely private on your device.

Rotation Angles Explained

  • 90° Clockwise (CW): Rotates the page a quarter turn to the right - useful for landscape pages that appear on their side
  • 180°: Flips the page upside down - useful for scans that came out fully inverted
  • 90° Counter-Clockwise (CCW): Rotates the page a quarter turn to the left
