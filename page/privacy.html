How we handle your files, data, and privacy at OnlineFileConv.com

At OnlineFileConv.com, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our online file converter and related services.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We respect your privacy and do not collect personal data such as your name, address, or contact information when you use our tools. We do not require registration, sign-in, or user accounts to access our file conversion services.

2. Uploaded Files

Files you upload for conversion are processed securely through our system. These files are stored temporarily only for the duration of the conversion process and are automatically deleted after completion. We do not access, share, or store your files permanently on our servers.

3. Log Data & Analytics

Like most websites, we may collect non-personal data such as browser type, device information, time spent on pages, and referral sources for analytical purposes. This helps us improve website performance and user experience. No personally identifiable information is included in this data.

6. Data Security

We use secure protocols (HTTPS) and server-side encryption to protect your files and browsing activity. However, while we take every measure to ensure safety, no online service can guarantee 100% data security. Uploading highly confidential or sensitive files is not recommended.

7. External Links

Our website may contain links to other external sites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such websites and encourage users to review their respective privacy policies before use.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store any data from minors. If you believe a child has submitted data to our platform, please contact us for removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update or modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. Continued use of our services indicates your acceptance of these updates.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices or wish to report a concern, please contact us at support@onlinefileconv.com.
